Explore Egypt

Cairo

Gateway to Antiquity

Cairo has been a major center of Islamic culture for centuries. Nearby Giza is home to the only remaining wonder of the ancient world: the Great Pyramid of Khufu.

Local Avian Life: Rose-ringed Parakeet

In Cairo, the Rose-ringed Parakeet is a common sight. These resilient birds thrive in the city's gardens and along the Nile, their bright green feathers contrasting with the desert-toned architecture.

Where to find: Look for them in the Al-Azhar Park or the gardens of the Gezira Island in the early morning.

Must-Visit Attractions

Pyramids of Giza

The iconic tombs of the Pharaohs, guarded by the Great Sphinx.

Egyptian Museum

Houses the world's most extensive collection of Pharaonic antiquities.

Cairo Gallery

Local Insights

🍲 Must-Try Dishes

Sample Koshary, Egypt's beloved national dish of lentils, rice, and pasta. For breakfast, try Ta'ameya (Egyptian falafel) and end your meal with Om Ali, a warm bread pudding.

🚗 Getting Around

Use Uber or Careem for reliable, fixed-price transportation. The Cairo Metro is an efficient way to bypass heavy traffic, though it can be very crowded during peak hours.
